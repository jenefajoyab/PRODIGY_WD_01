Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is a South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment. The group consists of 8 members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. They debuted on March 25, 2018. The group was created through the survival program with the same name, Stray Kids. As of February 10th, 2022, they are also signed under Republic Records.
